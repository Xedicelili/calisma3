Security cameras have been evolving at a rapid pace over the last two decades. From those clunky commercial security cameras from the early 2000s, they’ve become smaller, smarter, and able to make out incredible detail.


Nowadays, it’s commonplace to see cameras that can distinguish between people and other moving objects, detect packages, and even recognize faces. There are even some that can “talk” to ward off intruders even before they enter your home.
